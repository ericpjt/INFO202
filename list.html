About Me

Hi! I'm Eric. I'm a first-year master's student in the MIMS program at UC Berkeley's iSchool. Before becoming a UX Designer, I studied Russian Language in college and had enjoyed a wonderful year during my exchange program in Moscow. The pivot came during a student hackathon, where I discovered great passion in understanding and addressing people's needs. So I took a leap of faith into the world of UX. Plus, I'm a huge fan of Bauhaus ethos and minimalist design principles.
